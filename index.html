About Me

Cyberbotics Lab

I worked as a student research assistant in the Cyberbotics Lab at the Ohio State University from May 2019 to May 2021. During which I researched on perception, path planning and SLAM on this autonomous bipedal robot named Digit. I composed a thesis on global localization using visual odometry under the supervision of Dr. Ayonga Hereid in 2021.

More details coming soon!

Underwater Robotics Team

I joined the Underwater Robotics Team at the Ohio State University as a software team member in Jan. 2018. My main responsibilites were building a navigation and alignment system based on cameras and deep learning algorithms, and writing task codes in ROS for the competition. In 2019, 2020, I attended the RoboSub Competition in San Diego with my team, one of the largest underwater robotics competitions in the world.

More details coming soon!

Robomaster AI Challenge

I joined the RoboMaster Competition Team at the Ohio State University as a computer vision team member in Sep. 2019. I was elected to be the captain of the team in Nov. 2019. I led my team to compete in the 2020 RoboMaster AI Challenge, which is an autonomous multi robot competition held by DJI and ICRA. In 2020, I founded a student organization named Ultrabotics under OSU for my team.

More details coming soon!

MakeOHI/O Competition

I led a team of three to attend the 2020 MakeOHI/O Competition and came up with an easy, efficient visual solution to solve an IoT challenge proposed by Honda. My team won the 1st place in this challenge, and got invited to give a presentation of this project at Honda 99P Lab.
